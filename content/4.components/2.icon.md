## Available Icons

The `WangsIcons` type includes over 200+ icons covering common use cases:

### Navigation & Actions
- `add`, `close`, `edit`, `delete-bin`, `search`, `filter`
- `arrow-left`, `arrow-right`, `arrow-up-s`, `arrow-down`
- `menu`, `more`, `settings`, `refresh`

### Files & Documents  
- `file-add`, `file-edit`, `file-copy`, `files`, `folder`
- `pdf`, `csv`, `xls`, `doc`, `download`, `upload-2`

### Users & Communication
- `user`, `user-add`, `team`, `profile`, `mail`, `chat`
- `notification`, `bell`, `phone`

### Business & Finance
- `money-dollar-circle`, `coins`, `calculator`, `bank-card-line`
- `shopping-cart-2`, `store`, `receipt`

### System & Status
- `check`, `error`, `info`, `warning`, `success`
- `loader-4`, `spinner`, `time`, `calendar`

### Technology & Tools
- `database`, `cloud-line`, `qr`, `barcode`, `printer`
- `smartphone`, `device`, `tools`, `code-line`
